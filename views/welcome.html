Eduplex Sport’s facilities have been specifically designed with the aim of providing FAMILIES with the opportunity to relax in the outdoors during the week as well as over weekends. We cater for family fun including birthday parties, company and sporting events.

Our state of the art facilities includes our unique pump and mountain bike track – designed by Velosolutions. We provide safe walking and jogging routes. Other facilities include tennis and netball courts, cricket nets, cricket, hockey, rugby, athletics, softball and soccer fields. For a quick break or bite before the energy starts pumping we have a kiosk where water, cold drinks, energy drinks, coffee and health snacks can be bought.
